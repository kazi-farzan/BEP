ABOUT PROGRAM

June 2024 - August 2024
9 weeks duration

Target Audience:

Ideation or Early-stage Students/ Academicians/ Professionals.

Mode:

Hybrid

Bioriidl India's First DIY Biolab is organizing the 8th edition of Bio-Entrepreneurship Program (BEP), a 9-week program that will focus on ideation to prototype development and go-to-market strategies. The program will run from June to August 2024.

During the program, participants will be exposed to experts and mentors from technology, business, and design verticals. They will also learn how to do prototype development, market research, and prepare a business plan. At the end of the program, there will be a pitching session where teams will present their prototypes to investors. The winners will be selected based on their final prototype and the milestones they have achieved.
